Our Mission

Targest was created for the sole purpose of conducting requirements document management. What was already available in the market in terms of software tool wasn't as accessible in generating documents due to problems related to a bug that caused software to crash.

Since there was a need for this kind of software, we came up with a solution that will save companies money and time when it comes to handling requirements tracing.

TARGEST

Targest stands for Technical Abstraction Report Generator Extraction Software Tool.

Features

Easy to Use

TARGEST is designed with user experience in mind, making it easy to create and manage requirements documents efficiently.

Powerful Reporting

Generate comprehensive reports with TARGEST, helping you to track the progress and status of your work.

Automated Traceability Matrix Generation

Effortlessly generate traceability matrices to visualize the relationships between requirements, test cases, and other project artifacts.
